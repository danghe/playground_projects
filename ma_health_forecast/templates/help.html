Market Health (Macro)

Signal Detection
Methodology & Data Sources
Data Foundation (FRED)

We ingest real-time economic data primarily from the Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED) API. Key indicators include:

  • Interest Rates: 10-Year Treasury Constant Maturity Rate (DGS10)
  • Credit Spreads: ICE BofA US High Yield Index Option-Adjusted Spread (BAMLH0A0HYM2)
  • GDP Growth: Real Gross Domestic Product (GDPC1)
  • Market Volatility: CBOE Volatility Index (VIX)
M&A Health Index Algorithm

The "M&A Health Index" is a proprietary composite score (0-100) determining the favorability of the dealmaking environment.

Formula: We apply a Vector Autoregression (VAR) model to the historical time-series of the indicators above. The model calculates the impulse response of M&A volume to shocks in these macro variables.

"Higher Index = Lower Cost of Capital + Higher CEO Confidence + Lower Volatility"

AI Integration (Gemini 3.0 Pro)
Forecast Narrative & Executive Summary

While the VAR model provides the numbers, Gemini 3.0 Pro provides the story. We feed the raw forecast vectors and historical context into the LLM context window.

Utility: The Executive Summary explains why the index is moving. It connects a 50bps drop in credit spreads to a likely surge in LBO activity 6 months out. Use this to construct your pitch deck narrative: "The window opens in Q3."

Strategic Radar

Target Screening
Purpose & Components

The Strategic Radar acts as a pre-screening filter to identify "Deal Ready" companies before they officially hire an advisor. It continuously scans the coverage universe.

AI Brief & Sector Narrative

The "AI Brief" button triggers a real-time synthesis of the entire sector. It ingests recent news, earnings transcripts, and 10-K filings for the top 20 tickers.

  • Data Sources: Yahoo Finance News Stream, SEC EDGAR (indexed).
  • Output: A strategic memo highlighting sector-wide themes (e.g., "Consolidation in Mid-Cap Pharma").
Seller Propensity Index (SPI)

Score: 0-100

Drivers:

  • Activism: Recent 13D filings or proxy fights.
  • Stress: High Net Debt/EBITDA (>4x) or looming maturity walls.
  • Valuation: Trading >30% below 52-week high.
Actions: The AI Dossier

Each company row features an interactive "Action" menu. Selecting "Generate Dossier" prompts Gemini to build a 2-page tear sheet.

Executive Tip: The Dossier is designed to be copy-pasted directly into a pitch book. It generates "Potential Acquirers" and "Deal Rationale" automatically.

Deal Command

Execution
The Matchmaker Engine

Deal Command is the execution dashboard where Supply meets Demand. It takes the "Sellers" identified in Radar and matches them with capable "Acquirers".

1. Supply Side

Filters the Radar results into distinct "Playbooks":

  • Forced Sellers: Distressed assets.
  • Strategic Reviews: Board-initiated processes.
  • Carve-outs: Non-core divisions of Conglomerates.
2. Demand Side

Analyzes the "Buyer Universe" (Strategics & Sponsors) for:

  • Firepower: Cash + Debt Capacity.
  • Strategic Fit: Adjacency logic (e.g. Vertical Integration).
  • Dry Powder: PE Fund vintage and remaining capital.
3. The Match

Logic:

IF Seller = "Distressed" AND Buyer = "Turnaround Fund"

THEN Match Score = High.

Includes "AI Rationale" for every match.
